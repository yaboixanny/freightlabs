Common Questions About Warehousing Lead Gen

How long does it take to see results?

With paid ads (Google/LinkedIn), we typically see leads coming in within the first 2-3 weeks. SEO and organic strategies are longer-term plays that build momentum over 3-6 months.

What is the typical cost per lead?

For warehousing and fulfillment, qualified leads typically range from $80-$200 depending on the channel and competition. We focus on Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) to ensure the ROI makes sense for your margins.

Do you work with startups?

We work best with established providers who have product-market fit and operational maturity. If you are just opening your doors, we might recommend a focused Google Ads consultation rather than a full retainer.

What makes Freight Labs different?

We are specialists, not generalists. We know the difference between pallet-in/pallet-out and pick-and-pack. We don't waste your budget on keywords that attract irrelevant traffic.
